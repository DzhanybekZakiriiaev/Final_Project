Understanding AI Carbon Footprint
Data Center Impact

An average data center consumes about 460 TWh of electricity per year, globally, or roughly 1.4-1.7% of global electricity use. Which equals to the annual energy consumption of ~40 average homes [1]. This translates to approximately 300 metric tons of CO₂ emissions annually, the same as driving a car for 750,000 miles.

AI Model Training

"Training a single large language model like GPT-3 can emit as much as 552 metric tons of CO₂" [3], equal to driving a car for 1.4 million miles or the annual carbon footprint of 120 average people [4].

Everyday Comparisons
  • 1 Google Search [5] 0.2g CO₂
  • 1 Hour of Video Streaming [6] 36g CO₂
  • 1 Hour of AI Model Training [7] 284kg CO₂
  • 1 Data Center (Annual) [1] 300t CO₂
Note: These are approximate values based on industry averages. Actual emissions may vary based on energy sources, efficiency measures, and specific implementations.
